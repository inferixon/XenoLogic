# Xenopedia Aliens: Prompts for News Illustrations

# Xenopedia Image Prompts

## Image Spec

- **Target size:** `208 × 248 px`
- **Aspect ratio:** `26:31`
- **Orientation:** vertical portrait
- **Style:** photorealistic
- **Important:** **not** cartoon, **not** comic, **not** illustrated, **not** cel-shaded

## Global Art Direction

All Xenopedia creatures should feel like bizarre but believable alien specimen portraits from a strange research archive.  
The mood is absurd, eerie, slightly cute where appropriate, but still grounded in **photorealism**.  
They should look like real captured organisms, not mascots, not game icons, not concept art splashes.

### Core visual rules

- photorealistic alien creature portrait
- centered or slightly off-center subject
- clean readable silhouette
- strange but charming anatomy
- expressive texture detail
- soft cinematic studio or containment-lighting
- subtle sci-fi lab / enclosure / specimen context
- funny through form and expression, not through cartoon style
- absurd but plausible biological design
- suitable for a small Xenopedia card image
- high detail, sharp focus on creature
- simple uncluttered background
- no text, no labels, no frame, no UI
- no comic style, no anime, no toon rendering, no flat illustration

## Note

Because the final display size is very small (`208 × 248 px`), prioritize:

- strong silhouette
- readable face or front anatomy
- one clear emotional impression
- limited background clutter
- medium-close portrait framing
- visible texture and lighting over tiny detail noise
