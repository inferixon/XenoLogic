**Purpose**
Educational core: training of thinking, logic, inference, recognition of false conclusions.
A logic quiz where all the rules, hints, examples and lore revolve only around space creatures.
Game shell: strange space creatures, absurdity, humor, alien surrealism. lore-driven logic interface.

Baseline:
**No matter how strange the subject is, logic remains the same.**
XenoLogic is a sci-fi logic training interface where the player analyzes alien field reports and classifies what can be logically concluded.

three pluses at once:
-there are already ready-made mechanics;
-the topic becomes narrower and more original;
-this can be submitted as a site-archive /site-simulator /xeno-classification terminal, and not as a dry quiz.

**Plot**

Records of unknown life forms are collected at the research station.
Each question is a fragment of a field report.
You have to determine what logically follows from the creature's behavior.

**MVP Frame**

We need the following MVP scope, which:
-fully corresponds to Oppgave 2;
-already looks like a real product;
-does not create debt for later;
-but does not drag us into overdesign.

The MVP should include:
-Complete adapted logic system from the LogikkQuiz reference
-All logical operations that have already been developed
-The whole structure of difficulty levels
-The entire template-based generation pipeline
-Complete set of alien-only configs:
  -creatures in several categories/races
  -actions
  -properties
  -objects of interaction
  -auxiliary categories
-All system connectivity as a single generator-driven product
-10-15 consecutive questions with logical conclusions
-3 difficulty levels as in @LogikkQuiz
-No complex visual design (no sounds, animations, 3D graphics), only text interface like @LogikkQuiz

**Full Product Frame**
-Many classes of creatures with different actions and characteristics
-Each class has unique characteristics and behaviors that require different logic from the player.
-Additional levels of difficulty with more abstract and confusing creatures.
-Expanded lore and interactive elements such as audio-video recordings that add depth to the game world.
-More sophisticated visual design with more detailed UI and animations for each creature class.
